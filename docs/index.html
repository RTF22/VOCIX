Getting Started
How It Works

Three steps. No cloud. No subscription. No nonsense.

1
Pause
Hold to Record

Hold Pause from anywhere on Windows. The microphone activates instantly — VOCIX lives in the system tray and is always ready.

2
🎙
Speak Naturally

Talk normally. Release Pause when done. Whisper transcribes your speech locally — nothing ever leaves your machine.

3
⚡
Text Inserted

Processed text appears right at your cursor, in any application — email, browser, IDE, document. No paste required.

Intelligent Processing
Three Modes

Choose how your speech gets transformed before it hits the page. Switch modes instantly with keyboard shortcuts.

A
Clean
Ctrl+Shift+1  ·  Fully Offline

Strip filler words and light corrections — preserves your meaning, removes "um", "uh", false starts. Runs 100% locally, zero latency.

Before → After
"So um, I wanted to, uh, say that the meeting is… is tomorrow."
↓
"The meeting is tomorrow."
B
Business
Ctrl+Shift+2  ·  Claude AI

Rewrites your speech into professional, formal language suitable for email, reports, or client communication. Powered by Claude.

Before → After
"hey can you fix this bug it's been broken for ages"
↓
"I wanted to flag an ongoing issue that requires attention at your earliest convenience."
C
Rage
Ctrl+Shift+3  ·  Claude AI

De-escalates aggressive or frustrated speech into polite, firm communication. Preserve meaning, remove heat. Powered by Claude.

Before → After
"This is absolutely unacceptable, I'm furious about this delay!"
↓
"I'd like to express my concern regarding the delay and request an update."
